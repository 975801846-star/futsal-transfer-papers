| 字段 | 内容 |

| **作者** | Silva, Nakamura, Loturco, Ribeiro & Marcelino |
| **年份** | 2022 |
| **期刊** | *Science and Medicine in Football* (Taylor & Francis) |
| **DOI** | 10.1080/24733938.2022.2090600 |
