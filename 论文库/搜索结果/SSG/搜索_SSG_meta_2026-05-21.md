## 🔥 核心论文（打分 ≥ 8，共 15 篇）

### 🥇 战术约束（6 篇）

**1. Rumpf et al. (2026)** — *Man-marking, pressure on the ball, and offside and their effect on physiological, physical, technical and tactical parameters during small-sided games.*
- Frontiers in Sports and Active Living | PMID: 41695042 | Score: 12
- 叙述性迷你综述。Man-marking 增加生理负荷 (HR, RPE)，pressure on ball 提升跑动距离。
- ⚠️ 无定量合并 → meta 分析缺口。

**2. Rumpf et al. (2026)** — *Possession vs. goal scoring during small-sided games in soccer: a narrative mini-review.*
- Frontiers in Sports and Active Living | PMID: 41835029 | Score: 12
- Possession games: mean HR +4.35%, max HR +6.43%, >90%HRmax +39.7%
- Goal scoring games: 更高的 neuromuscular 负荷
- ⚠️ 无定量合并

**3. Qiao et al. (2025)** — *Higher Heart Rate Intensity Can Negatively Impact Tactical Decision-Making and Technical Accuracy in SSGs: Effects of Field Size and Scoring Method Manipulation.*
- JSSM | PMID: 40933338 | Score: 11
- 实验研究：pitch size × goal presence 交互效应对生理/技战术的影响

**4. Rumpf et al. (2026)** — *Manipulating player numbers in small-sided soccer games: effects on numerical superiority and inferiority.*
- Frontiers in Sports and Active Living | PMID: 42065084 | Score: 9

## 🦞 小龙虾推荐

**首选选题：触球限制 (touch limits) → 生理/体能负荷**

理由：
1. 文献量刚好（~12-15 篇原始研究）—— 不过多导致范围失控，不过少导致统计效力不足
2. 结局指标统一 —— 几乎所有研究都报 %HRmax、RPE、总跑动距离 → 可直接编码 SMD
3. Rumpf 2025 只有叙述性综述 → 定量 meta 分析是明确空白
4. 教练实操意义强 —— "限制触球次数到底会不会增加负荷？"是每个教练都会问的问题

**PICO:**
- P: 各年龄段/级别足球运动员
- I: 有限触球次数的 SSG (1-touch, 2-touch)
- C: 自由触球的 SSG (free play)
- O: %HRmax, RPE, 血乳酸, 总跑动距离 (TD), 高速跑 (HSR)
